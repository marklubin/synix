## Procurement Process

MegaCorp's procurement requirements:

1. Minimum 3 vendor bids required for any purchase over $200K
2. Security review by CISO office (2-3 weeks)
3. Legal contract review: 4-6 weeks after vendor selection
4. Procurement committee final approval: 1-2 weeks
5. PO issuance: 1 week after approval

## Success Metrics

MegaCorp has defined the following success metrics for the new platform:

- **User adoption**: 90% of licensed users active within 90 days of rollout
- **Report creation speed**: 30% faster than current Tableau workflow (baseline: 4.5 hours per report)
- **Data freshness**: real-time (sub-minute) vs current 4-hour refresh cycle
- **IT overhead reduction**: eliminate 2 FTEs dedicated to Tableau Server maintenance
- **ROI target**: positive ROI within 18 months, measured as cost savings + productivity gains
