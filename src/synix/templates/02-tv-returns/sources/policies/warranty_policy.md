# Warranty & Manufacturer Claims Policy

**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## Our Role in Warranty Claims

We are an authorized retailer, not the manufacturer. Our role in warranty claims is limited:

- **We do NOT process warranty claims or replacements on behalf of manufacturers**
- **We do NOT accept returns for defective items past our standard return window**
- We will provide proof of purchase documentation upon request to support your warranty claim with the manufacturer

## How Warranty Works

When you purchase a product from us, the manufacturer's warranty is between **you and the manufacturer**. If you experience a defect:

1. Contact the manufacturer directly (see your product documentation for contact info)
2. The manufacturer will troubleshoot, authorize a repair, or issue a replacement
3. If the manufacturer requires proof of purchase, contact us and we will email you a copy of your receipt
