# Acme Analytics — Competitor Profile

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Company Overview

Acme Analytics was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Austin, TX. The company raised a $60M Series C in September 2024 led by Summit Partners. Current estimated ARR is approximately $45M, up from $28M at the end of 2023. Total headcount is roughly 320 employees, with about 40 in engineering.

## Core Product

Acme's core offering is a real-time analytics dashboard focused on the mid-market segment (100-1,000 employees). The product emphasizes fast onboarding — their marketing claims "dashboards in 30 minutes" — and strong out-of-the-box visualization templates. They offer 50+ pre-built chart types and a drag-and-drop dashboard builder.

## Pricing

- **Standard**: $35/seat/month (billed annually), includes core dashboards and 5 data connectors
- **Enterprise**: $65/seat/month (billed annually), adds SSO, audit logs, and priority support
- No free tier; 14-day free trial available
- Volume discounts begin at 200+ seats (estimated 10-15% discount)

## Strengths

- Fast onboarding: customers consistently report going live in 1-2 days
- Strong visualization engine with polished, modern UI
- Good Salesforce and HubSpot native integrations
- Active community forum with 8,000+ members
- Responsive customer support with average 2-hour response time

## Weaknesses

- Limited REST API: only 40 endpoints, read-only for most resources
- No support for custom ML model deployment or bring-your-own-model
- Enterprise SSO implementation is unreliable — multiple customer complaints on G2 (3.2/5 for SSO)
- No on-premises or hybrid deployment option
- Data export limited to CSV and PDF; no programmatic bulk export
- Maximum 10 concurrent dashboard viewers on Standard tier

## Market Position

Acme holds approximately 12% market share in the mid-market analytics segment. Their sweet spot is companies with 100-500 employees that need quick time-to-value without heavy customization. G2 rating: 4.3/5 (680 reviews). Gartner Peer Insights: 4.1/5 (210 reviews).

## Recent Moves

- Launched an AI-powered "Smart Insights" module in Q3 2025 that surfaces anomalies automatically
- Hired a new VP of Sales from Looker in October 2025
- Opened a London office in November 2025 to expand EMEA presence
- Filed 2 patents related to real-time data streaming visualization in 2025
